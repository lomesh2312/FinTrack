## 🚨 Problem Statement

In real life, most people:

* Do not track their expenses properly
* Do not understand their financial condition
* Take loans without knowing the risk
* Get loan rejections without clear explanations

💭 Additionally, many people are unaware of important financial concepts like:

* Debt-to-Income Ratio (DTI)
* EMI burden
* Risk categories used by banks

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an **AI-powered financial management platform** that helps users:

* 📊 Track daily income and expenses
* 📈 Understand their financial health
* 🧠 Evaluate their **loan risk using AI**
* 🏦 Learn how banks assess their financial profile

It combines:

* A **full-stack expense tracking system (FinTrack)**
* An **AI-based Credit Risk Advisor**

👉 The system connects **real financial behavior** with **AI-driven decision-making**.

## 🎯 Solution

This platform provides a **simple and intelligent solution**:

1. 📊 Track income and expenses in one place
2. 📈 Analyze spending behavior
3. 🧠 Get AI-based credit risk prediction
4. 🏦 Understand how banks classify users into:

| Risk Level     | Meaning                      |
| -------------- | ---------------------------- |
| 🟢 Low Risk    | High chance of loan approval |
| 🟡 Medium Risk | Moderate risk                |
| 🔴 High Risk   | High chance of rejection     |
